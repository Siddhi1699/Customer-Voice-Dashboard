| **KPI**                           | **Description**                                          | **Calculation**                                                             | **Data Source**                         |
| --------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Total Reviews                     | Total number of reviews in dataset                       | `COUNT(review_id)`                                                          | amazon\_reviews.csv                     |
| Monthly Review Volume             | Number of reviews grouped by review month                | `COUNT(*) GROUP BY MONTH(review_date)`                                      | amazon\_reviews.csv                     |
| Avg. Rating per Product           | Average star rating per product                          | `AVG(star_rating) GROUP BY product_id`                                      | amazon\_reviews.csv                     |
| Sentiment Distribution (%)        | % of reviews that are positive, neutral, negative        | NLP classification counts / total reviews                                   | processed/sentiment\_analysis.csv       |
| Avg Sentiment Score over Time     | Trend of average sentiment score each month              | `AVG(sentiment_score) GROUP BY MONTH(review_date)`                          | processed/sentiment\_analysis.csv       |
| Top Positive Keywords             | Frequent keywords from positive reviews                  | TF-IDF or RAKE on sentiment = positive subset                               | processed/keyword\_extraction.csv       |
| Top Negative Keywords             | Frequent keywords from negative reviews                  | TF-IDF or RAKE on sentiment = negative subset                               | processed/keyword\_extraction.csv       |
| Negative Review Ratio per Product | % of negative reviews per product                        | `(Negative reviews / total reviews) * 100`                                  | sentiment join with amazon\_reviews.csv |
| Most Reviewed Products            | Products with highest review counts                      | `COUNT(review_id) GROUP BY product_id ORDER BY COUNT DESC`                  | amazon\_reviews.csv                     |
| Top-Rated Products                | Products with highest avg rating (with min review count) | `AVG(star_rating) GROUP BY product_id HAVING COUNT(review_id) >= threshold` | amazon\_reviews.csv                     |
